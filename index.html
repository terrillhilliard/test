Our story

About
Nón

Named after the Vietnamese conical hat, Nón Boutique Spa sits in the heart of An Thượng — Đà Nẵng's beachside quarter, moments from Mỹ Khê Beach. Our therapists blend Vietnamese tradition with modern technique in every ritual, from foot and hot stone massage to facials and nail care.

  • Authentic Balinese & Thai techniques
  • English-speaking, welcoming team
  • Couples & private rooms available
  • Open late, every day until midnight
